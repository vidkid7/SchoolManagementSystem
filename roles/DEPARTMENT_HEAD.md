## Permissions Summary

### Department Head Has:
- ✅ **Department-wide** oversight
- ✅ **Teacher supervision** capabilities
- ✅ **Performance monitoring** access
- ✅ **Report generation** for department
- ✅ **Curriculum oversight** authority
- ✅ **Communication** with department

### What Department Head CANNOT Do:
- ❌ Access other departments' data
- ❌ Modify student records
- ❌ Enter grades directly
- ❌ Access financial information
- ❌ Modify system settings
- ❌ Manage staff records
