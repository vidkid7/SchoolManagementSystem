## Workflow Examples

### Assigning Room to Student
1. Navigate to Room Management
2. View available rooms
3. Select appropriate room
4. Search for student
5. Assign student to room
6. Record check-in details
7. Notify student and parents

### Processing Leave Request
1. Navigate to Leave Management
2. View pending requests
3. Review leave details
4. Verify reason
5. Approve/reject leave
6. Notify student
7. Alert parents

### Recording Incident
1. Navigate to Incident Management
2. Click "Record Incident"
3. Enter incident details:
   - Date and time
   - Students involved
   - Description
   - Witnesses
4. Document actions taken
5. Notify authorities if needed
6. Follow up on resolution
