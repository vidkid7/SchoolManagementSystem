## Permissions Summary

### Accountant Has:
- ✅ **MANAGE** permission for finance operations
- ✅ **Full control** over fee structures
- ✅ **Payment processing** capabilities
- ✅ **Invoice generation** authority
- ✅ **Financial reporting** access
- ✅ **Payment gateway** management

### What Accountant CANNOT Do:
- ❌ Modify student academic records
- ❌ Access staff salary information
- ❌ Modify system settings
- ❌ Access other modules (library, ECA, sports)
- ❌ Delete payment records (audit trail)

## Workflow Examples

### Recording a Payment
1. Navigate to Payments
2. Search for student
3. Select invoice
4. Enter payment details:
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Transaction reference
5. Record payment
6. Generate receipt
7. Email receipt to parent

### Generating Invoices
1. Navigate to Invoices
2. Select class or students
3. Select fee structure
4. Set due date
5. Generate invoices
6. Review invoices
7. Send to parents via email

### Processing Refund
1. Navigate to Refunds
2. View refund request
3. Verify refund eligibility
4. Approve/reject request
5. Process refund
6. Generate refund receipt
7. Update records

## Best Practices

### Payment Processing
- Verify payment details
- Issue receipts immediately
- Reconcile daily collections
- Maintain accurate records
- Follow up on failed payments

### Invoice Management
- Generate invoices on time
- Send timely reminders
- Track invoice status
- Handle disputes promptly
- Maintain invoice templates

### Financial Reporting
- Generate reports regularly
- Review collection trends
- Identify outstanding fees
- Track payment methods
- Share reports with management

### Communication
- Send payment confirmations
- Follow up on overdue fees
- Respond to queries promptly
- Maintain professional tone
- Keep parents informed
