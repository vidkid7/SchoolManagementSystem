## Permissions Summary

### Student Has:
- ✅ **View access** to personal academic data
- ✅ **Submit** assignments
- ✅ **Enroll** in ECAs and sports
- ✅ **Communicate** with teachers
- ✅ **Access** learning resources
- ✅ **Download** certificates and documents

### What Student CANNOT Do:
- ❌ View other students' data
- ❌ Modify academic records
- ❌ Access administrative functions
- ❌ View financial details (except own fees)
- ❌ Access staff information
- ❌ Modify system settings
