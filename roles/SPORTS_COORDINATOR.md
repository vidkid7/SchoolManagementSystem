## Workflow Examples

### Creating a Team
1. Navigate to Team Management
2. Click "Create Team"
3. Enter team details:
   - Sport
   - Team name
   - Coach
   - Captain
4. Add team members
5. Save team

### Organizing Tournament
1. Navigate to Tournaments
2. Click "Create Tournament"
3. Enter tournament details
4. Add participating teams
5. Create match schedule
6. Publish tournament

### Recording Match Result
1. Navigate to Tournaments
2. Select tournament
3. Select match
4. Enter scores
5. Record player statistics
6. Save result
