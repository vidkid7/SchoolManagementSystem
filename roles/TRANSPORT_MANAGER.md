## Workflow Examples

### Adding a New Vehicle
1. Navigate to Vehicle Management
2. Click "Add Vehicle"
3. Enter vehicle details:
   - Registration number
   - Model and make
   - Capacity
   - Purchase date
   - Insurance details
4. Upload documents
5. Assign to route
6. Save vehicle

### Creating a Route
1. Navigate to Route Management
2. Click "Create Route"
3. Enter route details:
   - Route name/number
   - Start and end points
   - Pickup/drop locations
   - Timing
   - Capacity
4. Assign vehicle
5. Assign driver
6. Publish route

### Assigning Student to Transport
1. Navigate to Student Transport
2. Search for student
3. Select route
4. Set pickup/drop point
5. Confirm assignment
6. Generate transport card
7. Notify parents
